University Tribunal Member, starting July 2018

Office of Appeals, Discipline and Faculty Grievances - Toronto, Canada

I am being appointed to the University Tribunal as a Student Member for a two-year term starting July 1, 2018.

The University Tribunal plays an essential role in preserving the academic integrity of the University of Toronto while ensuring due process for those charged with offences. Allegations of academic misconduct are dealt with under the University's Code of Behaviour on Academic Matters. The University Tribunal, established under the Code, hears some of the most serious allegations of academic misconduct. When a hearing is required, a panel composed of a legally qualified chair, a faculty member, and a student member, hear and decide the matter. The lawyers who chair the hearings are senior members of the legal community.
