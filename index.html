ABOUT

Hi there! My name is Bradley and I'm currently a second year student in the business management co-op program at the University of Toronto.

I was recently appointed to the University Tribunal as a Student Member for a two-year term starting July 1, 2018. The University Tribunal is responsible for hearing and deciding allegations of academic misconduct.

I also recently completed my first co-op placement, where I was working in the Regulatory Affairs & Major Projects department at Hydro One Networks Inc. This department is responsible for preparing applications and obtaining authorization from quasi-judicial tribunals (such as the Ontario Energy Board and the National Energy Board) for major infrastructure projects, mergers & acquisitions, and service area changes.
