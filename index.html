ABOUT

Hi there! My name is Bradley and I'm currently a second year student in the business management co-op program at the University of Toronto.

I am currently at my first co-op placement, where I am working in the Regulatory Affairs & Major Projects department at Hydro One Networks Inc. This department is responsible for preparing applications and obtaining authorization from quasi-judicial tribunals (such as the Ontario Energy Board and the National Energy Board) for major infrastructure projects, mergers & acquisitions, and service area changes.

At school, I'm currently the Digital Solutions Manager at the Management and Economics Students' Association (MESA) after serving as an associate last year. I oversee MESA's website by keeping everything up to date and by creating content for students.
