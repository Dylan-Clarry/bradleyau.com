ABOUT

My name is Bradley Au and I am an adjudicator by trade. I have expertise in administrative law with experience on both sides of the fence - from hearing cases as a decision-maker in a tribunal to preparing submissions for cases before tribunals.

I am currently appointed as a Hearing Officer at the Trademarks Opposition Board. In this role, I am responsible for:

  • Rendering final decisions on matters under section 45 of the Trademarks Act and preparing written decisions with reasons;
  • Presiding at hearings and rendering oral rulings, including on procedural matters; and,
  • Preparing for hearings by reviewing evidence, written arguments, jurisprudence and by conducting legal research.
